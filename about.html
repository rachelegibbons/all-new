With over six years of experience in design, I am a seasoned designer with expertise in brand and UX work across a wide range of industries, including technology, entertainment, energy, and higher education.

A lifelong learner and student of design, I have worked to combine my vast design knowledge with the engineering world in order to bridge the gap between clients, designers, and engineers through a common vocabulary and in-depth industry experience. I am a Human Interaction Design Foundation certified UX Designer, and a graduate of the Epicodus night school program, through which I gained a deeper understanding of engineering technologies and processes, while honing my front-end development skills. Through my experience with these programs in combination with my design-based skillset, I am able to precisely and efficiently craft solutions for clients that both solve their core issues while simultaneously meeting their high-level business objectives.

Apart from design, my experience in a variety of industries has helped me to broaden my skills to encompass brand engagement, marketing and advertising, project management, UX and digital capabilities, people management, and leadership.

My primary passion is to provide helpful solutions in every context, while bringing an authentic design leadership voice to any team, no matter the size or setting.
